The science of mindfulness reveals that this ancient practice is more than just a trend; it is a powerful tool for rewiring the brain and improving mental and emotional health. By fostering changes in brain structure and function, mindfulness meditation enhances emotional regulation, reduces stress, and increases overall well-being.
